History

Puneet Maheshwari knew he had to start DocASAP when he needed to take his son to the emergency room for something that just required a doctor’s visit. The problem was that when he knew his son needed a doctor, he didn’t know how to find the doctors his son needed (ear doctor in this case). He knew that wherever people go to find a doctor, that’s where they should be able to book — embedded on the sites they already use. The right ear doctor should have practically found him!

While Puneet was a student in Wharton’s MBA program, he started DocASAP. It was not until a few years later, while working at McKinsey, that the timing was right to dive in with both feet. People were started to expect to be able to book doctors on the internet, and were less interested in waiting perhaps months to see their doctors.

Since then, DocASAP has skyrocketed, proudly seeing DocASAP’s booking capabilities on sites like WebMD, Magnacare, University of Pennsylvania Hospitals, Vitals, and Better Doctor. The entire team is extremely proud to see that early vision of medical access everywhere starting to becoming a reality.
